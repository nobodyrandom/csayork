Caribbean Students' Association - York University


The Caribbean Students’ Association (CSA) is a non-profit, student-volunteer and community oriented organization that acts as a representative body on behalf of the Caribbean student population at York University.
The CSA’s mandate embodies a mission that seeks to cultivate a spirit of unity, community and empowerment within the Caribbean community by centralizing the objectives, motives and efforts of Caribbean students into an organized, collectivist, student community federation.
